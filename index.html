— About Us

Who We Are

Team Javitron Racing is a Formula Student racing team from Ajeenkya D. Y. Patil University, Pune. We are passionate engineering students dedicated to designing, manufacturing, and racing go-karts. Built our first competition kart in just 1 month using CAD, FEA analysis, custom chassis design, and optimized powertrain engineering.

Pushing boundaries with cutting-edge engineering solutions and creative problem-solving.

— Our Process

The Journey

From concept to competition, our systematic approach to building championship-winning vehicles.

1

Design Phase

Concept development, CAD modeling, simulation, and design optimization. CFD and FEA analysis ensure performance before manufacturing begins.

3-4 Months
2

Manufacturing Phase

Fabrication of chassis, suspension, bodywork, and assembly of all components. Precision machining and quality control throughout.

4-5 Months
3

Testing Phase

Dyno testing, driver training, suspension tuning, and reliability testing. Data analysis and optimization for peak performance.

2-3 Months
4

Competition Phase

Static and dynamic events including design presentation, cost analysis, acceleration, skidpad, autocross, and endurance.

    — Our Story

    THE STORY OF JAVITRON

    From Passion to Performance

    2025

    THE BEGINNING

    It started inside Ajeenkya D.Y. Patil University, where a group of passionate engineering students decided to build something extraordinary.

    With no prior experience and limited resources, Team Javitron Racing took on the challenge of designing and building a competitive go-kart from scratch.

    2025

    THE BUILD

    In just one month, the team worked day and night on design, fabrication, and testing — transforming ideas into a fully functional race machine.

    2026

    THE BREAKTHROUGH

    At GKDC, the team entered as underdogs and achieved:

    • → 1st Position in Autocross
    • → 2nd in Design Evaluation
    • → 2nd in Acceleration
    • → Cleanest Pit Award
    • → All India Rank 6
    PRESENT

    THE FUTURE

    Today, Team Javitron Racing continues to grow — building better machines, competing at higher levels, and pushing the limits of student engineering.
